Spiced Lamb & Hummus Flatbreads

Description

For these tasy flatbreads, you'll spread creamy hummus over soft, warm tortillas, before topping with fiery spiced lamb, grated carrot and pickled shallot for a little zing.

Ingredients

  • 15ml red wine vinegar
  • 20g harissa paste
  • 390g chickpeas
  • 6 plain tortillas
  • 15ml tahini
  • 125g cherry tomatoes
  • 1tbsp ras el hanout
  • 1 spring onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 250g lamb mince
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 shallot

Steps

    • Boil half a kettle
    • Peel and finely slice the shallots
    • Dissolve 1tsp sugar in 1tbsp boiled water in a small bowl, then add the red wine vinegar with the sliced shallot and set aside to pickle - this is your pickled shallot
    • Meanwhile, drain the chickpeas
    • Peel and finely chop (or grate) the garlic
    • Preheat the oven to 200c/180c/gas 6
    • Tip: This is to warm your tortillas later - alternatively, add them to a plate and pop them in the microwave for 1-2 min!
    • Add the drained chickpeas to a food processor with the tahini and half of the chopped garlic (save the rest for later!)
    • Add 2 tbsp olive oil, 50ml cold water and a very generous pinch of salt
    • Blitz until smooth - this is your hummus
    • Tip: Add a small splash more water if your hummus is looking a little thick!
    • Heat a large, dry, wide-based pan (preferably non-stick) over a medium-high heat
    • Once hot, add the lamb mince and cook for 3-4 min or until browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as you go
    • Meanwhile, cut the cherry tomatoes in half
    • Add the tortillas to a baking tray and put the tray in the oven for 3-4 min or until warmed through (alternatively, add them to a plate and microwave!)
    • Once browned, add the ras el hanout to the lamb mince with the halved cherry tomatoes and cook for 2 min
    • Add the harissa paste (can't handle the heat? Go easy!) and remaining chopped garlic and cook for 2-3 min furhter or until the tomatoes are beginning to break down
    • Season with a generous pinch of salt and pepper - this is your spiced lamb
    • Top, tail, peel and grate the carrots
    • Trim, then slice the spring onion finely
    • Spread the hummus over the warmed tortillas
    • Top with the grated carrot, spiced lamb, sliced spring onion and pickled shallot
    • Enjoy!
